Speculative Design: Climate Change Chatbot

My team and I worked on a speculative design chatbot set in a world devastated by climate change. The chatbot aims to raise awareness about the potential impacts of climate change and promote understanding of survival strategies in extreme environmental conditions. By engaging in conversations about survival tactics and the consequences of climate change, the chatbot encourages users to reflect on current environmental policies and personal practices. This project combines interactive storytelling with an important social message, challenging users to consider the future of our planet.


Technologies Used:

  • OpenAI API: For generating conversational responses related to climate change scenarios
  • HTML: For creating the structure of the chatbot’s interface
  • CSS: For styling and designing the chatbot’s visual presentation
  • JavaScript: For handling user input and managing chatbot interactions
  • GitHub: For version control and collaboration
